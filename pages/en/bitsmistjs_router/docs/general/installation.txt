======Installation======

<WRAP admonition note links>
You need to import the core library before the router.
  *  <html><i class="fas fa-link"></i></html>[[en:bitsmistjs_core:docs:general:installation|BitsmistJS Core - GENERAL - Installation]]
</WRAP>

=====CDN=====

Load BitsmistJS Router from CDN in your html files.

<code html>
<script type="text/javascript" src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/bitsmist/bitsmist-js-router_v1@0.10.0/dist/bitsmist-js-router_v1.min.js"></script>
</code>

=====Download=====

Download BitsmistJS Router and put bitsmist-js-router_v1.min.js in the dist folder to somewhere under your websites and load it in your HTML files.

<code html>
<script type="text/javascript" src="/js/bitsmist-js-router_v1.min.js"></script>
</code>
